Story

A short VN about an OFW about to leave the Philippines.

Note: The game is written in Filipino. Because there is enough games written in English already.

We had two goals when we set out to make this:

  1. to make a horror game that does not rely on jumpscares
  2. to make a "news game" that is deeper than a simple action game.
